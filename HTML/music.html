ME AND MUSIC IN 21ST CENTURY

I was born in the first years of 21st Century so I had the chance to see the change of music through the years till now.
When I was a kid, I listened to Westlife, Backstreet Boys, Celine Dion, My Tam, Dan Truong.... Then when I was about 10 or 11, I came to Taylor Swift, SNSD, Big Bang, T-ara, Exo,...
There are so many talented singers, song writers, composers and so many awesome songs in this era. It's so lucky for us to live in such a franticly fast-growing music dynasty.
